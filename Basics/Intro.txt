Q. What is Python?
A. It is a high-level interpreted programming language known for its simplicity and readability. It was created by Guido Van Rossum in 1991.

Q. Why Use Python?
A.  -> Easy to Learn and Use
    -> Versatile and Powerful
    -> Large Community and Rich Ecosystem
    -> High Demand in Job markets (AI and Data Science)
